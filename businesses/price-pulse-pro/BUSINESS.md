## Summary

Price Pulse Pro is a competitive pricing intelligence platform that scrapes public competitor pricing pages daily, detects meaningful price changes, and pushes Slack alerts to revenue teams while exposing price history in a dashboard. It makes money by saving pricing analysts hours of manual checks, preventing silent undercutting, and justifying a monthly SaaS subscription for SMB and mid-market companies with 5–50 competitors to watch.
