# Business: Example AI Lead Scraper

- **Slug:** `example-ai-lead-scraper`
- **Status:** active (demo)
- **Owner contact:** Sheriff S → Business Manager Agent

## Summary

A demonstration compartment that proves the end-to-end Slick Enterprises HQ flow:
take an idea ("scrape AI startup leads and email a daily digest"), ask clarifying
questions, create the compartment, staff agents, run a task, and track cost.

> This is a **fake/example** business used for the v1 demo. It is not a real,
> revenue-generating business.

## Goal (v1)

Produce a daily digest of newly-discovered AI startup leads from approved sources.

## Target users

- The Owner (internal demo).

## Success metrics

- Demo passes the acceptance checklist in `docs/12-verification-guide.md`.
- A task is created, agents are shown, cost is tracked, artifacts exist.

## Scope (v1)

- In scope: compartment scaffold, agent team, one demo task, sample artifact.
- Out of scope: real scraping, real email delivery, real data.

## Risks & constraints

- Budget share: negligible (mock mode, $0).
- Legal/TOS: real scraping would require respecting robots.txt + TOS (see Scraper agent).
